Spec Mss Coll. - Mexico

1772

no. 657

Barrientos, Manuel (Vicar General and acting Archbishop)

A. L. S. Ms. D. S. 12 pp., sm. fol. 1772.

Dispensation of second degree of consanguinity for marriage denied to two Indians. The man had seduced the woman who was his niece. He was sentenced to 25 lashes every Sunday for four Sundays at the door of the Parish Church; to six months imprisonment; to pay the mother of his unborn child $25.00 and to see her no more.
